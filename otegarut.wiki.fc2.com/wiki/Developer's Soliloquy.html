2023/06/04

We have finally implemented the StageMOD ​​function.
You no longer need to be limited by the 3D models in-game.
(I can't set the lighting yet, so I guess the next step is lighting.)

In addition to that, we have changed the behavior of the car.
Up until now, when I was braking, I felt like the tires were locking up and I couldn't steer the steering at all.
There is a feeling of understeer and the car starts to turn a little.
It was a surprisingly controversial behavior, so I wonder what the reaction will be after the fix...

2020/02/15

It's been a long time since I've been alone.
I have sent the patch.
The number of active installs has increased, and I'm afraid to press the update send button.
(The maximum I've experienced so far is with 5,000 youtube subscribers, but all updates will be notified to everyone..)
By the way, about the content of the update that I'm interested in,
(1) Added Haruna to D-Touge mode (formerly known as Real Pass mode)
(2) Implemented grooving along with (1). I created a map chip for grooved curves.
(3) Changed the behavior of the car slightly to deal with the grooves in (2). I think the hill climb has become easier to run.

I really wanted to make a more "real" mountain pass,
In terms of physical strength and app capacity, I asked them to make it as an extension of LoadStage.
(That's why I named it like the pass that appears in xxxD. It's not real at all)
If someone gives me real mountain pass data, I'll replace it with that immediately ()

Haruna's roadside object is currently in production, so I think it will be a little better in the next update.
