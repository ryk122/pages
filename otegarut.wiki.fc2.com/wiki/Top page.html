Welcome to the official “Bad Boy Touge” Wiki!

This wiki is the official wiki for the car action game "Bad Boy Touge".
We send various information such as update information and game contents.
The Admin is a game developer named Ryk. Try to also look at Developer's Soliloquy(Japanese and English).

Downloads:

Android Version


PC version is not available.
however. By using an Android emulator, you can also play on a PC.
When playing with an emulator, you can use the arrow keys by selecting "Use controller" from the game app settings (Option).

Since the display is difficult to see on the smartphone version, it is recommended to use the PC version from the button on the upper right.
